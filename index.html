Vinícius Queiróz

Senior Product and multidisciplinary designer BASED IN CUIABÁ → brazil and currently working @Meiuca. focused IN INTERFACES, EDITORIAL DESIGN AND SIGNAGE.

This website was developed using simplified approaches with pure JavaScript (Vanilla JS), resulting in an intentionally minimalist implementation. The design process was carefully structured based on various influences and personal references, encompassing aesthetic and conceptual elements that have shaped different moments of my journey. Among the primary inspirations are retrofuturism, computer terminal interfaces, and the visual language of printed indexes. Additionally, the project's graphic composition strongly reflects the influence of contemporary studios and designers such as ID-C (id-c.se), Laura Jouan (laurajouan.fr), and Porto Rocha, whose innovative and experimental approaches resonate in the visual identity and interactive experience of this platform.
